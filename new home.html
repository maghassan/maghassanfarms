MAG HASSAN FARMS is a company that deals with the way farm products are cultivated and sales of agricultural products such as fresh cow milk (dairy milk), poultry (i.e. chickens, guinea fowl, etc.). MAG HASSAN FARMS also have some sub-system or branches that operate on veterinary services and pharmaceuticals services. It was founded in the year 1980 by ALH. MUHAMMAD GONI. It was first started with a small sales of products and now it has become a big company recognized worldwide. MAG HASSAN FARMS is located at Ribadu city of Fufore L.G.A, Adamawa state where all the farming and cultivation is done. Also the company has it head office at Yola north L.G.A of Adamawa where all the transactions are performed.
